Vietnamese restaurant

Inspired by Vietnamese street food. We opened the take away restaurant Hanoi Alley, in early 2022 in Nørrebro in Copenhagen. The small oasis is run by Thu Hien, an experienced restaurant owner from Vietnam, where she ran two popular restaurant chains Ngo 89 and 1976. Hanoi Alley offers an authentic and intimate environment – ​a cozy decorated restaurant with a small menu, where you will find the most authentic Vietnamese dishes, made with love and dedication.

See our menu here

Vietnamese street food expresses the beauty of the Vietnamese culture, lifestyle, feelings and thoughts. This is the foundation of the experience you will get at Hanoi Alleys, a rare breath of fresh air on the Copenhagen restaurant scene. To ensure that the taste remains fresh and authentic, our dishes are not made in advance. This also ensures to highlight the natural taste of the ingredients. You are greeted by a balanced flavor combination, highlighted by the contrasts of sour, spicy, salty and sweet, which helps to elevate the culinary experience. You can either eat in the restaurant or order take away. Call us to order your food or visit our restaurant in the heart of Nørrebro. We also offer catering for smaller parties if you miss the authentic taste of Vietnam for your party or dinner party. You can either be inspired by our menu on our website, or we will be happy to put together a special menu for you. Contact us today and hear more!
